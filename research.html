--- layout: page title: Research permalink: /research/ --- ANALYSES QUANTITATIVES DE LA MUSIQUE PAR DESMESURES REALISEES SUR DES SIGNATURES SPECTRO-TEMPORELLES DU SON (Conference Paper)

Abstract: The objective of this project is to develop tools based on an automatic analysis technique of musical extracts that we have developed and which makes it possible to characterize the sound qualities perceived on short duration samples, on a scale of a few seconds. This analysis is both a spectral and temporal analysis and allows to characterize a sound extract from the point of view of the overall perceived timbre in the short term as well as the variations over time of its energy flow. It represents its sound signature at a given moment. Different measurements have been made on signatures and allow to establish similarities and distances between signatures of the same piece of music or by comparing different musics. By operating on reference corpuses and performing automatic learning, we seek to realize different applications, concerning musicological analysis, recommendations for streaming and the development of new sound synthesis techniques.
